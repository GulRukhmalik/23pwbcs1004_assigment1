Nutrition Guide

Empowering you with knowledge on meal planning and healthy eating habits!

Meal Planning

  • Plan meals around protein-rich foods, whole grains, and colorful vegetables.
  • Prep ingredients in advance to save time.
  • Stick to balanced portions of macronutrients: carbs, fats, and proteins.

Healthy Eating Habits

  • Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Eat slowly and mindfully.
  • Reduce sugar intake and avoid processed foods.

Recipes

  • Breakfast Smoothie: Blend spinach, banana, almond milk, and chia seeds.
  • Grilled Chicken Salad: Toss mixed greens, grilled chicken, and a balsamic dressing.
  • Energy Bites: Mix oats, peanut butter, and honey; roll into bite-sized balls.
